The Standard Group

Known in the Middle East as specialist suppliers for Pumps, Motors, Compressors and systems, the Standard Group is dedicated to providing timely and well-engineered fluid solutions to almost every market segment!

The Standard Group, was founded by Jabir Rasheed, a Mechanical Engineer and Entrepreneur, who started Standard Ship Spares LLC in 1986 to procure and supply emergency spares and marine equipment to the thriving marine and ship repair industry in the UAE at that time. Through a strong network of worldwide suppliers, SSS succeeded in growing the organization by meeting urgent customer demands swiftly and competitively.

The Standard Group offers a wide range of technologies by internationally acclaimed manufacturers with a customers throughout the GCC, Middle East and Indian subcontinent.
